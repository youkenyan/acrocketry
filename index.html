About Algonquin College Rocketry

ACR is a multidisciplinary team that aims to design and build a rocket that will reach Mach 2 and 22,000 feet. Our team is constantly improving our skills in various fields including mechanical, electrical, robotics, and computer engineering, as well as business, marketing, and graphic design.

We're competing in the Basic Category of the 2025 Launch Canada Competition against universities across Canada, with plans to participate in Launch Canada's Advanced Category and the Spaceport America Cup in the future.

Our Mission & Goals

Short Term Goals

  • Design, test, build, and successfully launch our rocket
  • Reach a launch apogee of over 22,000ft and Mach 2

Medium Term Goals

  • Advance to higher levels of the Launch Canada competition
  • Expand our outreach efforts
  • Enhance our team self-sufficiency

Long Term Goals

  • Compete in future Launch Canada Competitions
  • Participate in Spaceport America Cup
  • Inspire the next generation of STEM professionals
